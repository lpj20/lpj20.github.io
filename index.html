Peijun Li 李沛君

I am a Master's student at Inregrated Circuits and Systems Group  at Tsinghua University, fortunately working with Prof. Chun Zhang and Zhihua Wang. Before entering Tsinghua, I was an undergraduate student in Tianjin University of Technology, major in Microelectronics Science and Engineering.

My current research interests lie in the areas of hardware accelerator, hardware-software co-design, computer vision, deep learning, VLSI and robotics.
